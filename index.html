Task Abstraction

High level: Discover

Mid-level: Locates

Low level: Compare

The visualization is being used to discover information about current temperature trends in comparison to the past. This visualization locates. The target is the temperatures from the last few days and it locates where they are in order to see if the current temperature trends are out of the ordinary. This visual is comparing specific points to the larger trend to show how current temperatures are not the norm. Overall, the visual aims to identify recent temperature trends compared to the past.

High level: Present

Medium level: Lookup

Low level: Summarize

Target: Overall, this visualization demonstrates how many days there have been extreme temperatures. It is presenting the set facts of how many days have been 20 degrees higher than average. The mid-level task is to lookup as the target (days 20 above the average) and location (when they occurred) are known. Finally, it summarizes these facts because it is displaying the general information about days over 20 with multiple days in multiple cities highlighted.
