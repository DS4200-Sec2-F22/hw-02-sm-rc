Task Abstraction

High level: Discover

Mid-level: Locates

Low level: Identify

The visualization is being used to discover information about current temperature trends in comparison to the past. This visualization locates. The target is the temperatures from the last few days and it locates where they are in order to see if the current temperature trends are out of the ordinary. This visual is identifying specific points out of a larger trend to show how current temperatures are not the norm. Overall, the visual aims to identify recent temperature trends compared to the past.

High level: Discover

Medium level: Locate

Low level: Identify

Target: Overall, the visual aims to identify occurrences of extermemely high temperatures. It discovers days where the temperature was over 20 degrees above the average. The target is known (days with unusually high temperatures), but the location (when they occurred) is unknown, which means the mid-level task is to locate. Finally, it identifies these occurences.
